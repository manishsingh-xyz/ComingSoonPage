ANHADTM

Powering Limitless Learning
Passionately committed to being the harbinger of change in the way education is delivered and consumed in the 21st century.

We are a diversified product based ed-tech start-up helping private education institutions become sustainable centres of excellence by helping them lower the cost of student acquisition, improve digital infrastructure and provide top-tier educational platforms and products as well as placement support for their students.

We are solving the problem of access to and quality of education through pioneering & innovative models and world-class products.

AnhadTM education ecosystem includes a student centric platform for online college admissions, an employability enhancing platform for having English conversational classes, a virtual school as well as virtual placement support. Other verticals include Apple IDUP - Mac Research & Innovation Centre Set-up, technology - enabled education, online education software, training and human resource management, English Language Training, Life Skills Programme with schools and Virtual Classroom solutions.

  • Our Vision:

    To solve the problem of access to and quality of education.



  • Our Mission:

    To bring affordable, accessible, engaging and effective education to underserved, underwhelmed, misguided but hungry learners.
